    How do I Access Form 1098-T through one.ou.edu?

    The 1098-T (Tuition Statement) is a Federal tax form from OU to report payments received by or made by students. In order for a student to access their 1098-T Form, they will need to log in with Single Sign-On at one.ou.edu:

    1. Under the Financial Tab, click Bursar. 
    2. Click the “ Download 1098-T” link.
    3. Log in to Single Sign-On again. Then, click “View” in the 1098-T field under Statements.

    If you are having an issue logging into ONE with your OUNetID, please see this article.
