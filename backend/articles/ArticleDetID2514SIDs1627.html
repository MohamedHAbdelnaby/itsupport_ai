    MyMedia - ADA Compliant Captions

    While Kaltura MyMedia has AI captions automatically added to any video uploaded, they do not meet the ADA required percentage of accuracy. The University of Oklahoma has a contract with a vendor to provide ADA level captions. If you have a request for ADA compliant captions on a video associated with a student, please have them fill out the request form from the Oklahoma Accessibility and Disability Resource Center.

    If you have a non-course related video that needs ADA captions for public use, please email hessman@ou.edu for pricing and instructions on how to order captions via MyMedia. Please know billing information will be required to order captions.
