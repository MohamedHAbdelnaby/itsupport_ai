    How do I make an advising appointment?

    f you are an OU student looking to make a advising appointment, you may do so at the one.ou.edu site.

    1. To begin, navigate to one.ou.edu in your internet browser and log in.
    2. Next click the Academics tab.
    3. Now click the Advising tab and then Schedule advising appt. button.
