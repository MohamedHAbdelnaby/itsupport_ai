    Class Roster Photos

    Access to The Book is through https://sis.ou.edu/thebook

    If your are an instructor and wish to view pictures of the class roster and you have access to The Book, you  can do so as follows:

    1. Log into Canvas
    2. Click on “The Book”
    3. Select the term the course is in and then select the course.
    4. Click “View Course Roster”.

    Doing it through The Book will put up the information on file for your students.
