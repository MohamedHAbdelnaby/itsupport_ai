    OU Software Catalog

    The University offers a wide range of free and for-purchase software for work and school use. A variety of operating systems and desktop applications such as Adobe Creative Cloud, MATLAB, and Windows are listed in our Software Catalog. For each item in the catalog, you can view information about its purpose and features, qualified users, directions for download and/or purchase, and support options.
