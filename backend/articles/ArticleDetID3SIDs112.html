    IT Maintained Lab Software List

    For a listing of software available in OU IT maintained computer labs, please see this page. On the linked page you will find a listing of software grouped by academic area, which includes:

    • All Areas – All IT maintained labs
    • CEE – College of Earth and Energy maintained labs
    • COA – College of Architecture maintained labs
    • COE – College of Engineering maintained labs
    • iHub – 3 Partners Place Innovation Hub

    For additional labs information, please visit their web page.
