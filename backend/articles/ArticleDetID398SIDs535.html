    Canvas – Adding the OU Writing Center Page into your Canvas Course

    As an instructor, you can add the OU Writing Center page to your course.

    Open Settings

    In course Navigation, click the Settings Link.

     

    Navigation


    In course Settings, click the Navigation tab.

    Drag and Drop


    In course Navigation Tab, drag and drop the OU Writing Center tab from the disabled pages list to the course navigation list above.

    Save and Publish

    In course Navigation Tab, click save and you should have the OU Writing Center Tab added to your Navigation SideBar.
