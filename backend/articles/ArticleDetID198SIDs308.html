    Faculty/Staff OU Address Book Phone Number Update

    If you are a current OU faculty or staff member and you wish to update your OU phone number, you may do so through two methods:

    Employee Self Service

    • Navigate to https://selfservehc.ou.edu/.
    • Log in using your OUNet ID and password.
    • Click your portrait in the upper right corner and select Account Settings.
    • In the Phone Numbers section, you may enter in your Campus, Home and Cell number.
    • The OU search.ou.edu People Search list and the Global Address Book in Outlook will update with your new phone information within 24 hours.

     

    ONE.OU.EDU

    • Navigate to one.ou.edu and log in using your OUNet ID and password.
    • Click to expand the “EMPLOYEE ADDRESS AND PHONE NUMBERS” section.
    • In the Contact section, click Edit next to the Campus, Home, Main or Other number you wish to change. You may also select which to list as your preferred number.
    • Once your are done, you may click Save Changes.

     

    Other Pages

    • The OU search.ou.edu People Search list and the Global Address List in Outlook will update with your new phone information within 24 hours.
