    Removing Media from MyMedia

    Remove a video from MyMedia

    • Log in at mymedia.ou.edu with your OUNetID and password.
    • Click Your User Tab on the top right of the home screen.
    • Select My Media from the dropdown menu. This will bring up a list of your personal uploads.
    • Next to the video click the Delete option.
    • Click Yes to confirm deletion.
    • This can be done in bulk by checking multiple videos on the My Media page and clicking Delete.
