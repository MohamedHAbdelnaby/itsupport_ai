    Removing a Participant from a Zoom Meeting

    Identify the participant who is disrupting the meeting

    Scroll to the meeting management ribbon and click “Manage Participants”

    If the disruption needs to be terminated immediately, simply click “Mute all” located at the bottom of the participants pane.

    Find the disruptive participant in the participants lists, hover over their name, with the cursor, and hit the “More” option to remove the participant

    Confirm this is the participant you want to remove and click “Okay”

    The User will not be able to rejoin the current meeting
