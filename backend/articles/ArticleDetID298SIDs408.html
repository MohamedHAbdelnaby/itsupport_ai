    Adding MyMedia to Canvas Course Navigation

    1. Navigate to your Canvas course and click on the Settings button located on the bottom of the left side menu.
    2. Click on the Navigation tab located at the top of the page.
    3. Scroll down until you see the MyMedia tool, click on the three dots, then select Enable.
    4. You should now see the MyMedia tool in the top list on the page. You can drag and drop the menu items in the order of your preference.
