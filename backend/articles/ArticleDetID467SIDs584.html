    How Do I Access Sooner Card Information through ONE?

    In order to access Sooner Card information through one.ou.edu:

    1. Under the Financial tab, click “Sooner Card”.
    2. Under the Sooner Card tab, there are multiple options including your balance, meal point information and reloading your Sooner Card.
