    Using Blackboard eAccounts for iOS

    eAccounts is a versatile mobile app that can be used to view your account and board plan balances along with recent usage, make a deposit to Sooner Sense for on-campus purchases and add your Sooner Card to Apple Wallet on iPhone and Apple Watch.

    Here’s what you need

    • Your eligible device.
    • iOS 12 and WatchOS 5.
    • PingID for two-factor authentication.
    • Transact eAccount for iOS.

    Using eAccounts for the first time

    1. Download Transact eAccounts from the App Store
    2. Select University of Oklahoma
    3. Select Norman Campus from the campus list
    4. Login using your OU Net ID and password. This is the same username and password used for other OU sites.
    5. Choose your preferred PingID two-factor authentication method.

    Viewing account balances and activity

    After logging in, a list of assigned accounts, along with their current balance, will be shown on the eAccounts main page. Follow these steps to view each account’s recent activity in detail.

    1. Tap on the account you would like to view in more detail.
    2. On the Transaction Activity tab, you will find the location and dollar amount of your most recent payments.

    Viewing board plan balances and activity

    After logging in, a list of assigned board plans, along with their current balance, will be shown on the eAccounts main page. Follow these steps to view each board plan’s recent activity in detail.

    1. Tap on the board plan you would like to view in more detail.
    2. On the Activity tab, you will find the location of your most board plan transactions.
    3. On the Remaining tab, you will find the number of meals remaining for the current week.  Board plans with a set number of meals per week will reset each Sunday morning.

    Making a deposit to Sooner Sense1

    Sooner Sense is an included declining balance account that allows your Sooner Card to be used like a debit card for on-campus purchases wherever Sooner Card payments are accepted. Follow these steps to make a deposit.

    1. You’ll make your first deposit by visiting Sooner Card Online – https://ou.edu/soonercard/online
    2. You can use Apple Pay as an easy and secure way to make deposits.
    3. At the end of the deposit you will be asked to save the payment method.
    4. Name the card being used and select both checkboxes:
      • Save payment method
      • Set card as default

    Adding your Sooner Card to Apple Wallet, iPhone

    1. Tap the “Add to Apple Wallet” button. The icon to the left will be replaced by your photo after provisioning.
    2. Select your iPhone.
    3. Tap Next. You will need to agree to the Sooner Card Terms and Conditions.
    4. Your Sooner Card will be added to Apple Wallet and ready for use.

    Adding your Sooner Card to Apple Wallet, Apple Watch

    1. Tap “Add to Apple Wallet” to the right of “Apple Watch” on the eAccounts main page.
    2. Select your Apple Watch.
    3. Tap Next. You will need to agree to the Sooner Card Terms and Conditions.
    4. Your Sooner Card will be added to Apple Watch and ready for use

    Still have questions?

    Give us a shout! Sooner Card is here to help. You can email us at soonercard@ou.edu or call our office at 405.325.3113. If you prefer to talk in person, visit our locations in the Oklahoma Memorial Union or Stubbeman Place.

    1. Sooner Sense is an optional declining balance account that can be used for on-campus payments wherever the Sooner Card is accepted
